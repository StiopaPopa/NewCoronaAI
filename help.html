Frequently Asked Questions

What is CoronaAI.lu?

CoronaAI.lu is an information hub powered by artificial intelligence (AI) technology designed to simplify the way information on Coronavirus as relates to Luxembourg can be found. Whenever you type in a query into the search bar, the search request is analysed using AI technology. The website then displays the search results almost instantly. Please note that CoronaAI.lu can only be used for information on Coronavirus as regards Luxembourg. Any statistics or other information relating to other countries are not searchable on CoronaAI.lu.

Why use CoronaAI.lu?

Although there are other sources of information on Coronavirus in Luxembourg, the unique search technology used by CoronaAI.lu simplifies the search process in that users can use free-style searches, and the trained AI retrieves relevant results. CoronaAI.lu is not designed to replace other sources of information on Coronavirus in Luxembourg. The purpose of CoronaAI.lu is to provide an effective tool which pulls together information from other sources and simplifies the search process.

How to search for information on CoronaAI.lu?

Simply type your query into the search bar and press the enter/return key on your keyboard. The search results will be displayed right below the search bar.

What information can I search for?

You can search for almost anything relating to the Coronavirus, as regards Luxembourg. The latest statistics, the COVID-19 symptoms, the locations and the opening hours of testing and vaccination centres, and other relevant information can be readily found on CoronaAI.lu.

Are the search results current?

The search results reflect the information as it gets released / posted by the Government of Luxembourg, including information posted by the Luxembourg Ministry of Health, other governmental bodies, and the Covid19.lu website.
